BloodReign

Blood BloodReign

This game was made in 2018/2019 at Ontario Tech University by a group of 6 students. The goal of the game was to use it to get into Level Up Showcase (an event for schools to show off their students best video games). This game can be played by 2-4 players in a last man standing style gamemode. Each player can play any of the 4 different characters each with their own special abilities.
